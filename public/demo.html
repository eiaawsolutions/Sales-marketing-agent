Lead scoring with reasoning.

Every lead ranked 0–100, BANT-explained

How it works

Each inbound lead is analysed across profile, company size, title, engagement, behaviour. The agent assigns a score from 0–100 and a BANT reasoning paragraph (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline). You read why, not just what.

What you see

Lead cards show score + qualification status + recommended next action. Hot leads (80+) flag for attention so focus stays where it matters.

Outreach sequences, written for you.

Multi-step email campaigns with tracking
+

AI-generated sequences

Give the agent a lead profile — it generates a 4–5 step outreach plan: email subjects, message bodies, follow-up timing, channel mix (email, LinkedIn, call). Each step personalised to the lead's industry and pain points.

Campaign automation

Create campaigns, assign leads, send with tracking. See open rates, click rates, replies in real time. The agent suggests optimal send times and subject-line variations.

Visual content

Branded posters and social graphics via AI image generation. Choose style, add headlines, export ready-to-publish visuals. No design skills needed.
